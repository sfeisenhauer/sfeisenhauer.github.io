Recent Research






My recent research examined contextual development and well-being at a Green Door Theatres (Pseudonym) after-school program in urban Los Angeles. Along with traditional ethnographic methods, I introduce the use of electrodermal activity a psychophysiological response as part of a qualitative protocol rather then its traditional quantitative use. The research spanned one school year with follow up visits still taking place now. The thriving community at Green Door Theater welcomed me through their open doors as much as they welcome the children and youth they serve.
